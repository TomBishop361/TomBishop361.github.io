Pressure Point

Embark on a perilous journey into the depths of the unknown in this immersive iron-punk sci-fi adventure. Set in a dystopian future where the rusty waters of a desolate planet conceal dark secrets. As a convicted criminal offered a chance at redemption, you find yourself sealed inside a one-man submarine, your mission is clear: repair and maintain this vital vessel as it plunges into the treacherous depths in search of crucial resources. Your skills as a mechanic will be put to the ultimate test as you face relentless challenges and unforeseen obstacles. From patching leaks to turning valves, every repair is a race against time as you strive to keep your submarine operational in the face of ever-present danger. The fate of your mission rests solely on your ability to keep your submarine afloat in the face of adversity.
